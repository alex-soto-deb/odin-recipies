Chilli con carne

Ingredients

  • 1 Large onion
  • 1 Red pepper
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 Heaped tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 Tsp papria
  • 500g Lean minced beef (I personally prefer beef and pork mix)
  • 400g Chopped tomatoes
  • 410g Can red kidney beans
  • Plain rice to serve
